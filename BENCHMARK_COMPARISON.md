## Strengths & Gaps Analysis

### Fatiha Strengths (Competitive Advantages)

1. **Gamification Leadership** — Only platform with XP, levels, badges, streaks, combos
2. **Offline Capability** — Works without internet, no server needed
3. **Privacy** — No accounts, no tracking, no data collection
4. **Accessibility** — Verified WCAG 2.1 AA with documented audit trail
5. **Unique Content** — Evidence reversal, CAST, AI in meta-analysis, prognostic reviews
6. **Cost** — Completely free, no paywalls, no "premium" tiers
7. **Self-Contained** — Single HTML files, portable, easy to distribute
8. **Open Source** — Can be forked, modified, translated

### Fatiha Gaps (Areas for Improvement) — V8.0 Update

1. ~~**No Certification**~~ ✅ **RESOLVED** — Downloadable HTML/PDF certificates with XP, badges, level
2. ~~**No Network Meta-Analysis**~~ ✅ **RESOLVED** — Advanced course Module 1 "The Network" covers full NMA
3. **No Health Economics Module** — Cochrane has this (potential future addition)
4. ~~**Limited Language Support**~~ ✅ **RESOLVED** — Now **12 languages** (exceeds Cochrane's 11)
5. **No Live Instructor Support** — JBI and FREE Synthesis Training offer live workshops
6. **No Peer Review** — Coursera has peer assignments
7. **No CME/CPD Credits** — Some platforms offer continuing education credits

The Synthesis Course Collection is **competitively positioned** against major evidence synthesis training platforms. Its unique combination of:

- **Comprehensive gamification** (unmatched in the market)
- **Complete accessibility** (WCAG 2.1 AA verified)
- **Zero cost** (no paywalls, no subscriptions)

- ✅ Certificate generation (downloadable HTML/PDF)
